About Me

My name is Terri and welcome to my website! This site is here to show my personal work (which is also on my Instagram page ‘terrishires’).

My interest started at school with techniques like screen printing, batik and dying. From there, I branched into printing illusions; a theme which is reoccurring throughout my work!

Continuing with textiles, I attended university and became interested in woven textiles. My portfolio shows an exploration journey into handwoven tapestry weaving, demonstrating the tactile nature of landscape art.
